Lasagna

Description

A simple lasagna recipe for those who can't be bothered to slave over the oven.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: You can use ground turkey for a lighter option.
  • Spaghetti sauce: Use either store bought or home made.
  • Cheeses: You'll need cottage cheese, mozerella, and Parmesan
  • Eggs: These help to bind the cheese mixture together
  • Seasonings: Season the lasagna with dried parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna sheets: Again either store bought or home made is fine
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking.

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozerella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna in layers>
  4. Bake covered for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
