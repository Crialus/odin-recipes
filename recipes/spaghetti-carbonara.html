Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A 5 ingredient spaghetti carbonara.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound guanciale (cured pork cheek), diced
  • 16 oz pack spaghetti
  • 3 large eggs
  • 10 tablespoons grated Pecorino Romano cheese, divided
  • sald and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the porn in olive oil until browned and crispy, then drain on paper towels
  2. Boil the spaghetti in salted water. Drain and return it to the pot. Let cool
  3. Whisk the eggs, 1/2 of the cheese, and some pepper in a bowl until smooth.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy.
  5. Stir in the pork, then top with the remaining cheese and more black pepper.
