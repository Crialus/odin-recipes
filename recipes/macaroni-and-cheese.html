Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A simple mac and cheese recipe for those who can't be bothered to slave over the oven.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni: Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni until al dente, about 8 minutes
  2. Make a Roux: While the macaroni is cooking, go ahead and start on the roux. Start by melting butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper, stirring until smooth. Slowly pour in milk and stir until the mixture is smooth and bubbling. Be careful not to let the milk burn.
  3. Add Cheese and Macaroni: Add cheese. Slowly stir in Cheddar cheese until smooth and melted. Grated cheese is recommended. Once the macaroni is finished cooking, drain and stir into cheese sauce until coated.
